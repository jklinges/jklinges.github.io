Humidity (%) vs. Latitude

Cities in the dataset experience humidity between 0% and 100%. There are a few cities in the Northern Hemisphere where the humidity is 0%. While there is no one-to-one association between the humidity and latitudes, there is a somewhat symmetrical distribution of cities in both hemispheres for humidity between 60% and 100%, and between 20% and 60%. In the polar regions, temperatures are low and relative humidity is high. Specific humidity is at a maximum in the Tropics and declines as one moves poleward reaching minimum values in each polar region.
